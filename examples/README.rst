Worked examples
===============

These examples are intentionally small and physics-facing.

- The plane-wave examples plot diagonal form factors and selected
  exchange-kernel channels.
- The radial plotting scripts give quick visual checks for form factors
  and exchange kernels.
- ``compare_exchange_backends.py`` compares the available exchange
  backends directly.
- The pseudopotential example plots a standard lowest-Landau-level
  Coulomb benchmark.
- ``print_macdonald_ritchie_tableII.py`` prints a package-based
  reproduction of MacDonald-Ritchie Table II and documents the
  paper-to-package normalization map.
